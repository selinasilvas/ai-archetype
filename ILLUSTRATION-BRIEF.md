# AI Archetype Quiz — Illustration Brief

## Overview

17 illustrations total: 11 for the user archetypes (Mode 1) and 6 for the AI relationship profiles (Mode 2). These will replace the emoji placeholders in the live tool, appear on shareable result cards, and potentially become standalone prints/social assets.

## Design Direction

**The core tension to capture:** This is a tool about the relationship between humans and AI. The illustrations should feel unmistakably *human-made* — your hand-drawn, colored-pencil-and-Procreate style is the entire differentiator. Every other AI-related tool on the internet looks like it was designed by AI. This one should look like it was made by an artist who thinks deeply about technology.

**Tone:** Warm but not cutesy. Playful but with substance. Think editorial illustration meets character design — each archetype should feel like a person you might know, not a mascot.

**Style notes:**
- Recommend a consistent character style across all 17 — similar proportions, similar level of detail — so they read as a cohesive set
- Could be full figures, bust/portrait, or a mix — your call based on what lets you show the most personality
- Background elements or objects can do a lot of storytelling (what's on the desk, what's in their hands, what's in the environment)
- Color palettes for each are specified below — these match the UI, so try to incorporate them as dominant tones

**Format:** Each illustration will be used at two sizes:
- **Card size:** ~120x120px in the results UI (needs to read clearly at small scale)
- **Share card:** ~200x200px area in the generated social image
- **Optional hero:** Larger for potential landing page, Substack, or print use

Recommend working at a large resolution and designing with the small-scale readability in mind — clear silhouettes, distinctive shapes, limited fine detail.

## Mode 1: The 11 Archetypes

Each archetype represents a way of *relating to AI* — not a job title or personality. The illustration should capture the energy and values of the person, not literally depict them using a computer.

---

### 1. The Executor ⚡
**Color palette:** #FF6B35 (warm orange), #FFB088 (peach)
**Tagline:** "Ship it. Now."
**Energy:** Velocity, decisiveness, no-nonsense efficiency
**Visual direction:** Someone in motion. Could be mid-stride, mid-gesture, already looking past the thing they just finished. The environment around them might show completed tasks, checkmarks, a trail of shipped work. There's a sharpness and forward lean to their posture.
**Key feeling:** You blinked and they already moved on.

---

### 2. The Deep Diver 🔬
**Color palette:** #2D6A4F (forest green), #95D5B2 (sage)
**Tagline:** "But what does the data actually say?"
**Energy:** Focused intensity, patience, precision
**Visual direction:** Someone fully absorbed. Could be surrounded by layers of information — papers, screens, books, magnifying elements. The environment has depth, literally and metaphorically. Maybe we see them at the bottom of a deep space, or peering through something. They're not rushed; they're thorough.
**Key feeling:** They won't surface until they've found the real answer.

---

### 3. The Spark Plug ✨
**Color palette:** #E63946 (red-coral), #FFB4A2 (soft pink)
**Tagline:** "What if we tried...?"
**Energy:** Effervescent, generative, slightly chaotic in a good way
**Visual direction:** Ideas radiating outward from them — sparks, lightbulbs, sticky notes, connecting threads. Their expression is animated, mid-thought. The space around them is alive with possibility. Not organized — energized.
**Key feeling:** Their excitement is contagious.

---

### 4. The Strategist ♟️
**Color palette:** #1D3557 (navy), #A8DADC (cool teal)
**Tagline:** "Let's think three moves ahead."
**Energy:** Calm authority, seeing the whole board
**Visual direction:** Someone looking at a larger picture — could be a chess-like setup, a map, a systems diagram. They're not panicked; they're reading the field. There's a sense of elevation — they see from above. Composed posture, thoughtful gaze.
**Key feeling:** They already know what's going to happen next.

---

### 5. The Builder 🏗️
**Color palette:** #7B2D8E (purple), #D4A5E5 (lavender)
**Tagline:** "Let's make this real."
**Energy:** Hands-on creation, iteration, momentum
**Visual direction:** Actively constructing something — tools in hand, surrounded by components, partially-built structures, sketches becoming real objects. The process is visible — this isn't a finished product, it's mid-build. There's sawdust/paint/code/material in the air.
**Key feeling:** They can't stop building even if they wanted to.

---

### 6. The Navigator 🧭
**Color palette:** #BC6C25 (amber), #DDA15E (warm gold)
**Tagline:** "Help me figure out what I'm actually dealing with."
**Energy:** Wayfinding, clarity-seeking, gentle determination
**Visual direction:** Someone at a crossroads or looking at a complex landscape, finding their bearings. A compass element works literally. The environment could be a bit misty or complex, and they're the calm center of it. Not lost — orienting.
**Key feeling:** They'll find the way, but they want to understand the terrain first.

---

### 7. The Polymath 🌀
**Color palette:** #6A0572 (deep purple), #C77DFF (bright violet)
**Tagline:** "It's all connected, actually."
**Energy:** Fluid, multidimensional, integrative
**Visual direction:** This is the most complex one visually. Multiple domains flowing into and out of this person — could be represented as swirling threads, overlapping worlds, tools from different disciplines coexisting. They're the point where everything converges. Not overwhelmed — synthesizing.
**Key feeling:** Their mind is a room with too many interesting doors, and they've opened all of them.

---

### 8. The Apprentice 🌱
**Color palette:** #0077B6 (bright blue), #90E0EF (sky blue)
**Tagline:** "Teach me how this works."
**Energy:** Open, curious, absorptive, fresh
**Visual direction:** Someone looking up or leaning in with genuine wonder. Books, questions, growth imagery (the sprout/seedling metaphor works here). They're collecting — gathering information, filling up, growing. There's a lightness and openness to their posture.
**Key feeling:** Every answer generates two more questions, and they love it.

---

### 9. The Conscientious Objector ⚖️
**Color palette:** #9B2226 (deep red), #E8998D (rose)
**Tagline:** "Just because we can doesn't mean we should."
**Energy:** Principled, watchful, grounded
**Visual direction:** This person stands firm. They might be facing a wave/force/current and not moving. Or holding up a hand — not in fear, but in deliberate pause. A scales/balance element works. They see clearly and they're choosing not to go along. Dignified, not angry.
**Key feeling:** Someone you'd want in the room when important decisions are being made.

---

### 10. The Reluctant Adopter 🌊
**Color palette:** #457B9D (slate blue), #A8C5DA (light blue-gray)
**Tagline:** "I'll use it... but I have feelings about it."
**Energy:** Ambivalent, thoughtful, honest about the tension
**Visual direction:** Someone with one foot in, one foot out — literally or metaphorically. Maybe they're holding something technological with a contemplative expression. There's a push-pull visible. Not resistant, not enthusiastic — genuinely navigating the middle.
**Key feeling:** They're not confused. They're holding complexity that others oversimplify.

---

### 11. The Boundary Setter 🚪
**Color palette:** #344E41 (dark green), #A3B18A (olive/sage)
**Tagline:** "This — yes. That — absolutely not."
**Energy:** Deliberate, clear, self-possessed
**Visual direction:** Clear delineation — a person with a defined space. Could be a door they've chosen to open (or close), a garden with a clear fence, a desk with very intentional items. Everything in their world is there by choice. Clean, composed, purposeful.
**Key feeling:** Their clarity is a superpower.

## Mode 2: The 6 Relationship Profiles

These are about the *dynamic between human and AI* — so they should feel relational rather than individual. Could be depicted as two figures, a person with a mirror/reflection, or more abstract/metaphorical.

**Visual approach suggestion:** These could be more abstract than the archetypes — looser, more atmospheric, using the "between" space as the subject. If the archetypes are character portraits, these could be relationship portraits.

---

### 12. The Co-Creative Partnership 🎭
**Color palette:** #6A0572, #C77DFF
**Energy:** Two forces creating together, riffing, building on each other
**Visual direction:** Two entities in creative dialogue — could be passing something between them, building something together, or both contributing to the same evolving creation. Equal energy, mutual engagement.

---

### 13. The Thinking Mirror 🪞
**Color palette:** #457B9D, #A8C5DA
**Energy:** Reflection, clarity, seeing yourself more clearly
**Visual direction:** A person and their reflection — but the reflection reveals something the person couldn't see alone. The mirror isn't a copy; it's a clarifying lens.

---

### 14. The Expert on Call 📡
**Color palette:** #2D6A4F, #95D5B2
**Energy:** Precision, expertise, clean signal
**Visual direction:** A clean transmission — signal flowing clearly between two points. Could evoke a radio tower, a precise instrument, a focused beam. Functional beauty.

---

### 15. The Gentle Challenger 🌿
**Color palette:** #BC6C25, #DDA15E
**Energy:** Warmth with honesty, growth through friction
**Visual direction:** A growing thing meeting gentle resistance — a plant finding light through structure, two figures in respectful debate, a hand holding something carefully but firmly.

---

### 16. The Reliable Tool 🔧
**Color palette:** #FF6B35, #FFB088
**Energy:** Clean utility, no pretense, functional
**Visual direction:** An elegant tool — well-worn, well-used, respected for what it does. No ornamentation. Could be a hand holding something purposeful, or a single well-crafted instrument.

---

### 17. The Expedition Partner 🗺️
**Color palette:** #E63946, #FFB4A2
**Energy:** Shared adventure, curiosity, discovery
**Visual direction:** Two figures looking at the same horizon, setting out together, following a map to unknown territory. There's energy and openness — they don't know where they're going, and that's the point.

---

## Production Notes

**File format:** PNG with transparency, minimum 1200x1200px working resolution
**Naming convention:** `archetype-executor.png`, `profile-cocreator.png`, etc.
**Deliverables per illustration:**
- Full-resolution source (Procreate file or high-res PNG)
- Cropped/optimized version at 400x400px for the web tool
- Isolated version (no background) for the share card overlay

**Integration:** Once illustrations are ready, the emoji references in `src/data/archetypes.js` get replaced with image imports, and the ShareCard component gets updated to render illustrations instead of emoji. Happy to handle the code side whenever you have assets ready.

---

*Every position on the spectrum is a legitimate one. There's no wrong way to relate to AI — only unexplored ones.*
